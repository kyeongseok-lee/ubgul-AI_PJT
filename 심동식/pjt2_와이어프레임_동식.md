##### 화상미팅

![미팅](pjt2_%EC%99%80%EC%9D%B4%EC%96%B4%ED%94%84%EB%A0%88%EC%9E%84_%EB%8F%99%EC%8B%9D.assets/%EB%AF%B8%ED%8C%85.jpg)
